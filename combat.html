Important Combat Basics

Weapons

Weapons are used to fight against other players and NPCs. There are low tier weapons which can be crafted witout learning them and they range up to high end weapons which can destroy heavily geared in a matter of seconds.
Read More

Armour

Armour is used to protect yourself from attacks of other players and NPCs. Low end armour can only withstand a few shots of a low powered gun but high end armour can stop a sniper rifle. Armour can also provide protection from radiation or cold.
Read More

Medical

Healing items are very important as during a fight, if you are not shooting and are behind cover, they can be used to restore your health back to full. They are essential when fighting in monuments against NPCs which come in waves.
