Types of Mid-Tier Raiding

Satchels and
Beancan Grenades

The first type of explosives that is avalible to be crafted. It only requires a workbench level 2 but requires more sulfur to craft in comparison to explosive ammo.

Explosive Ammo or
Silent Raiding

Explosive Ammo is the best way to raid smaller bases during the middle of the wipe. It is much quieter than other methods of raiding and also cheaper. It requires additional rifles to shoot the explosive ammo but Semi Auto Rifles can be used making the additional cost lower.
