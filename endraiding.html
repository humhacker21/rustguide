Types of End-Game Raiding

C4 "Drilling"

In a heavily fortified base, C4 is used to "drill" to the core or near the core so rockets can be used. They are faster than rocket raiding and are the fastest way of raiding but are inefficient as they do not have area damage.

Rocket Raiding

Rocket Raiding is considered the best way to raid large bases as it creates an area damage. It has a high cost as rockets require a launcher to fire but can demolish a base in seconds. They can also be used from a distance so they can be launched from a raid tower to decimate a base before having to enter an enemy compound.
