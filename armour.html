Armour

Most Cost Effective:

Hazmat Suit

Hazmat suits are commonly found around the map and are relativly cheap in comparison to other similar armour. They also have many benefits such as radiation protection and water resistance.

Best:

Metal Facemask/Chestplate

The only armour that can stop a sniper headshot from instantly killing you, this is the best armour but it has an extremly high cost to make.
