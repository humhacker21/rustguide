Medical/Healing

Most Cost Effective:

Pumpkins

Although they are a food item and not a medical item, pumpkins heal 10HP everytime they are eaten which can be done at a fast rate. If you can find a river, many pumpkins can be found there which can be very useful in the early game.

Best:

Medical Syringe

Syringes are the best way to heal in a fight as they instantly heal 15HP and then an additional 20HP will slowly recove if you do not take damage. Due to the ability to heal you instantly means they are the best thing to use in a fight.
