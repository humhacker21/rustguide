Efficient Raiding Guide

Eco Raiding

Eco Raiding is the use of many cheap items such as spears or eoka pistols to break through low tier building blocks such as wooden doors and stone walls that were placed backwards.

Mid Tier Raiding

Mid Tier raiding is the most common type of raid and is usually done by groups of 2-4 players and is cost effective. Satchels or explosive ammo is commonly used.

Endgame Raiding

Raiding using rockets, C4 and auto turrets is usually only done by large groups who have high level gear and is usually done to be as fast as possible sacrificing efficiency.
