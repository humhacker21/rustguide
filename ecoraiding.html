Types of Eco Raiding

Soft-Side Raiding

When the back of a stone or wood wall is exposed, it can be easily destroyed using pickaxes, spears or swords. This is useful for getting into a bases shooting floor as you can destroy a stone ceiling through shooting windows.

Eoka Raiding

The Eoka pistol is a cheap shotgun pistol and while it is ineffective at combat, it can be used to shoot through wooden doors or walls. This means it is very useful at the start of a wipe as you may not have blueprints but the Eoka can be used to raid.
