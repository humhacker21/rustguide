Weapons

Most Cost Effective:

Semi Automatic Rifle

The Semi Automatic Rifle (SAR) is cheap to make, has a long range and moderatly high damage at the cost of a slower rate of fire.

Best:

AK-47 Assault Rifle

The Ak-47 Assault Rifle is the best all round weapon in Rust. It is capable of killing high level players within a few seconds and can be used at close to mid range and at longer ranges if the user is skilled.
